PRIVACY NOTICE — Routinely (a habit-tracking app)
Effective date: March 2025

ABOUT THIS NOTICE
This notice explains how Routinely handles your personal data when you use our
mobile application.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We collect the habits you choose to track, your daily check-in entries, your email
address, and your time zone. We use your time zone to schedule reminder
notifications at appropriate local times.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
We use your data to operate the habit-tracking features, send you reminder
notifications, and improve our app through aggregated usage analysis. We rely on
your consent, given when you create an account, as the legal basis for sending
reminder notifications. We rely on the necessity of performing our contract with
you for the core habit-tracking functionality.

SHARING YOUR INFORMATION
We use a third-party email delivery service to send account-related emails. We use
a third-party push-notification service to deliver reminders to your device. Both
providers are contractually restricted from using your data for their own purposes.

SECURITY MEASURES
All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols. We perform
regular security reviews of our infrastructure.

CHANGES TO THIS NOTICE
We may update this notice from time to time. We will notify you of material changes
via email or an in-app notification.

CONTACT US
If you have questions about this notice, email us at hello@routinely-example.com.
