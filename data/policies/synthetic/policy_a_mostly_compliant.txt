PRIVACY POLICY — CloudNote (a note-taking app)
Last updated: January 2025

1. WHO WE ARE
CloudNote ("we", "us") provides a cloud-based note-taking application. We are the
data controller for personal data processed through our service. You can contact
our Data Protection Officer at dpo@cloudnote-example.com.

2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT
We collect your email address and name when you create an account. We collect the
content of notes you create, store, and share through the app. We collect device
information (browser type, operating system) and IP address for security purposes.

3. WHY WE PROCESS YOUR DATA
We process your account information to provide the note-taking service to you,
which is necessary for the performance of our contract with you under Article
6(1)(b) GDPR. We process device and IP information based on our legitimate interest
in securing our service against unauthorized access, under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.

4. HOW LONG WE KEEP YOUR DATA
We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active. If you
delete your account, we will erase your personal data within 30 days, except where
we are required to retain certain records for tax and accounting purposes for up to
7 years, as required by applicable law.

5. WHO WE SHARE YOUR DATA WITH
We share your data with our cloud hosting provider, which stores your notes on our
behalf. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share aggregated,
anonymized usage statistics with analytics partners; this aggregated data does not
identify you personally.

6. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
Our hosting provider operates servers exclusively within the European Economic Area.
We do not transfer your personal data outside the EEA.

7. YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data. You have the
right to restrict or object to our processing of your data. You have the right to
lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. To exercise
any of these rights, contact us at dpo@cloudnote-example.com.

8. SECURITY
We encrypt your data in transit and at rest. We restrict access to personal data to
employees who need it to perform their job duties.

9. MARKETING COMMUNICATIONS
With your separate, explicit consent, we may send you marketing emails about new
features. You may unsubscribe from these emails at any time using the link provided
in each email, free of charge, with no impact on your use of the core service.

10. CHILDREN'S DATA
CloudNote keeps a permanent record of all user activity, including for users under
the age of 16, with no special parental consent process, because notes data is
considered low-risk.
