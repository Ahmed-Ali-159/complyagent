PRIVACY POLICY — SnapDeal Express (a flash-sale shopping app)
Updated: 2024

WHAT WE COLLECT
We collect your name, email, phone number, home address, payment card details,
browsing history within the app, precise GPS location at all times the app is
installed (even when not in use), your device contacts list, and your photo
library metadata.

WHAT WE DO WITH IT
We use your data however we see fit to grow our business. We share and sell your
personal data, including your location history and contacts list, to advertising
partners, data brokers, and any other third party willing to pay for it. We do not
ask for your permission before sharing this data.

HOW LONG WE KEEP IT
We keep all data forever, including after you delete your account, in case we need
it for future business purposes.

YOUR CHOICES
You have no ability to access, correct, or delete your personal data. By using this
app, you waive any rights you may have under any data protection law, including the
GDPR.

CHILDREN
This app is intended for all ages, including children under 13, and we collect the
same data from child users as from adult users with no additional safeguards or
parental consent.

INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
We transfer all data to our servers and to our advertising partners' servers
worldwide, including in countries with no data protection laws, with no additional
safeguards.

SECURITY
We do not encrypt stored data, as encryption slows down our systems.

CONTACT
We do not provide a contact method for privacy inquiries.
